Sofi Baby Tracker - Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 22, 2026

Introduction

Sofi Baby Tracker ("we", "our", or "the app") is a baby tracking application designed to help caregivers monitor and log their baby's daily activities. We are committed to protecting your privacy and the privacy of your family.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information:

  • Account Information: Email address for authentication purposes
  • Baby Profile Data: Baby's name, date of birth, and optional profile photo
  • Tracking Data: Feeding times and amounts, sleep sessions, diaper changes, pumping sessions, growth measurements, and tummy time sessions
  • Household Data: Information about caregivers within your household who you invite to share access

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to:

  • Provide the baby tracking functionality of the app
  • Sync data between caregivers in the same household
  • Authenticate your account
  • Send transactional emails (magic link login, password reset)

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted cloud database provider. We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Row-level security ensuring you can only access your household's data
  • Secure authentication with email verification

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties.

Your baby tracking data is only accessible to:

  • You (the account holder)
  • Other caregivers you explicitly invite to your household

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: Database and authentication (Supabase Privacy Policy)
  • Google Sign-In: Optional authentication method (Google Privacy Policy)
  • Apple Sign-In: Optional authentication method (Apple Privacy Policy)

Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

Children's Privacy

While our app is designed to track information about babies and children, we do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children. All data is entered and managed by adult caregivers.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
